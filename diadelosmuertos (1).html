Dia de los Muertos

What is Dia de los Muertos?

Dia de los Muertos or Day of the Dead, is a Latin American holiday honoring the deceased by many. This holiday is celebrated from October 31 to November 2.

How is it celebrated?

Deceased loved ones put up altars with photos, meaningful objects, and their favorite foods to attract their souls. Bright yellow-orange marigold flowers are put as a path to guide their souls from the cemetary. As tradition people make and put skulls and pan de muerto on altars. People paint their faces with a calavera makeup that looks like a human skull and dress in colorful clothing.
